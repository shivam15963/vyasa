About VYASA

Vyasa stands for"Guru" in Sanskrit.As education plays a vital role in educating individuals within society, to prepare and qualify them for work in economy as well as to integrate people and teach them values and morals of society.Our main motive is to unite such people who are passionate for teaching and those students who can't afford thier education.
These students can find an available teacher in thier area.Vyasa motto is to provide education to one and all so that we can learn and grow collectively..

Our Vision

"Vyasa" aspires to be nationally acclaimed website which inspires youth and transform the life of these students so they can help in nation building.
